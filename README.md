# 🏠 Global Airbnb Market Pulse Dashboard

**Tools:** Excel · Power BI · Data Visualisation | **Type:** Training Project — Data Visualisation & Business Intelligence | **Scope:** 279,712 listings · 10 Cities · 5.37M+ Reviews

---

## 📌 Project Overview

This project analyses **279,712 Airbnb listings across 10 global cities** to uncover market trends, pricing dynamics, host behaviour, and customer review patterns — built as a multi-page interactive Power BI dashboard to support **data-driven hospitality and investment decisions**.

> **Key Finding:** Paris, NYC, and Sydney account for nearly **48% of total reviews**, while **Mexico City and Rio de Janeiro** rank as the highest-rated cities globally. Hotel rooms price at **$800 average** — nearly double Entire Place listings at $673.

---

## 🎯 Business Questions Answered

- How has Airbnb's listing growth evolved from 2008 to 2020 — and how did COVID-19 impact it?
- Which cities dominate market share and cumulative listings?
- What are the average prices across property types — Hotel Room, Entire Place, Private Room, Shared Room?
- Which cities have the highest and lowest guest ratings?
- How frequently do customers leave reviews, and what seasonal patterns exist?
- What percentage of hosts are verified — and how does trust signal affect listings?

## 🔍 Methodology

1. **Data Cleaning** — Handled nulls, standardised city names, formatted date fields in Power BI
2. **Data Modelling** — Relationships built between Listings and Reviews tables
3. **DAX Measures** — Calculated KPIs: cumulative %, avg price by property type, trust signal ratios
4. **Dashboard Design** — 3-page interactive Power BI report covering Growth Trends, Market Share & Pricing, and Reviews & Trust
5. **Insight Delivery** — Business narratives written directly on each dashboard page for stakeholder clarity

---

## 💡 Key Insights

- **2015** was Airbnb's peak year for new listings globally — followed by a decline due to tightening local regulations
- **COVID-19 (2019–2020)** caused the sharpest drop in new listings across all property types
- **Paris** leads all cities in total listings and reviews — Hotel Room prices there average **2x the Airbnb rate**
- **Mexico City (94.8)** and **Rio de Janeiro (94.6)** are the highest-rated cities; **Hong Kong (89.7)** and **Istanbul (91.1)** are the lowest
- **86.3% of reviewers** wrote only one review — indicating mostly one-time guests
- **66.9% of hosts** are fully identity-verified with a profile picture — building platform trust
- **Paris and Rome** dominate review activity from April to August — peak European summer travel season
- **New York** sees review spikes in November and December — holiday season travel demand
